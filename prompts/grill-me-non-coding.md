# Grill-Me: Non-Coding Variant

Decision tree exploration for strategy, hiring, GTM, product decisions, or any non-code decision. Adapted from Matt Pocock's viral grill-me skill.

## The Prompt

```
Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one by one. For each question, provide your recommended answer — when the recommendation is obvious, I'll just say "yes" and we move on.
```

## When to Use

- Planning a go-to-market strategy
- Making a hiring decision
- Designing a course or workshop
- Evaluating a business model
- Any decision with multiple interdependent sub-decisions

## What to Expect

A 20-45 minute session with 15-50 questions. The AI walks every branch of your decision tree, surfacing dependencies and forcing clarity on things you haven't fully thought through.

The non-coding variant removes "explore the codebase" — it's pure decision tree exploration.
